KILLER HOP

An unapologetically rowdy American Pale Ale

Here's to those who won't take no for an answer


We've cranked up the flavour by adding 3 types of aromatic hops in hedonistic quantities with a light malt bouquet brought in for backing. Expect gentle waves of light honey, biscuit and caramel from the special malts with an explosion of tropical fruits for fireworks.

Stage dive into our Killer Hop APA like there's a thousand hands to catch you and you'll never look back as you crowd surf into the sunset.

THE GUZZLER

A thirst crushing Pilsner

Here's to the days where nothing can quench your thirst.

The Guzzler is brewed to be light, complex and layered. Just like rocking it out in flowering fields filled with summer, the fresh grassy, floral and slight lemony aroma will satisfy the greatest thirst with crisp bitterness, refreshing carbonation and lingering malty sweetness on the palate.
Gone are the days of boring one dimensional Pilsners. Walk tall, think big and lead the pack.

TRUE GRIT

A "never say die" Amber Ale

Here's to our brewmaster's legendary father who devoted 9 years to building an aeroplane in his garage - only to crash it during his test flight. He is currently building Mark II. We salute you, sir.

Medium bitterness, like that first, failed attempt followed by the sweet biscuit, honey, caramel and toffee flavours of success followed by citrus and fruity hop aromas of everlasting glory.

An elegant beer that slows down time for a quiet contemplation around the drawing board as you plot your flight plan to success.

ELECTRIC LIGHT

A hard rocking Weiss Beer.

Here's a real crowd pleaser for every mood. A three-chord classic of malt, yeast and hops composed with low bitterness and no regrets.

Freshly-baked bread and slightly malty sweetness are provided by the fine ensemble of malts in the verse and floral, gentle clove, citrus and fruit aromas in the chorus.

With it's crisp, spicy and tropical fruit overtones, Electric Light was brewed to kickstart your heart. Press "Play" and enjoy this classic on infinte repeat.
